Our mission

Empowered Future is a non-profit organization dedicated to the cause of supporting the education of at-risk teens & adolescents by offering them opportunities and gateways to education that may have been previously inaccessible to them.

Empowered Future’s mission is to help families in disadvantaged socioeconomic statuses, especially migrant families, bolstering their children’s education through charitable resources in search of a better, empowered future.

About Us

who we are

Empowered Future was created by a group of passionate high school students motivated to inspire change. The organization hosts events, fundraisers and educational programs to bolster the support of at-risk children and help them to succeed.

what we do

Empowered Future receives outside funding from sponsors, other local organizations, and fundraising from the generosity of anybody willing to help support the cause. All proceeds go to funding our programs and events.
